Privacy Policy

App: Color Crash Racer • Developer: Devolox Software Solutions • Last updated: October 5, 2025

We care about your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it’s used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • No personal information is collected directly (such as name, email, phone).
  • Device and usage data via third-party SDKs:
    • Advertising (Google AdMob): may collect device identifiers, ad performance data, coarse location, and crash/diagnostic signals to show and measure ads.
    • Analytics (Firebase Analytics) (if enabled): anonymous usage and engagement metrics (e.g., sessions, screens, events).

2. How We Use Data

  • To operate the app, improve performance and stability.
  • To show, cap, and measure advertisements.
  • To understand aggregated app usage trends.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (Ads) – see Google’s Privacy & Partners policies.
  • Firebase (Analytics/Crashlytics) – see Firebase Privacy & Security.

These providers may act as independent controllers of some data they process. Please review their policies for details and opt-out options (for example, device ad settings).

4. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above. Aggregated analytics may be kept longer for trend analysis.

6. Your Choices

  • You can reset your device advertising ID in system settings or limit ad tracking preferences.
  • You can uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers outside your country. Appropriate protections are applied where required.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.
